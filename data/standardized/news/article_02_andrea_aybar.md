## Detailed Non-Verbatim Brief

Theo VietnamMedia/TTXVN, ngay 10/11/2024, PC04 Cong an TP HCM dang dieu tra vu Andrea Aybar cung mot so nguoi nghi lien quan den viec to chuc su dung chat ma tuy. Bai viet xac dinh Andrea Aybar la nguoi mau, dien vien An Tay, ten Nguyen Thi An, sinh nam 1995.

Dien bien bai bao neu rang chieu 9/11, cong an kiem tra mot can ho tai chung cu cao cap o TP Thu Duc. Tai dia diem nay, luc luong chuc nang phat hien Andrea Aybar cung mot so ban be co bieu hien to chuc su dung ma tuy. Ket qua test nhanh cua cong an duoc bai bao neu la Andrea Aybar duong tinh voi chat ma tuy.

Bai bao ket luan rang cong an dang tiep tuc mo rong dieu tra va chua cong bo ket qua xu ly tai thoi diem dang tin. Vi vay, khi dung tai lieu nay trong generation, can phan biet giua giai doan dieu tra ban dau va cac thong tin khoi to/xu ly co the xuat hien sau do o nguon khac.
